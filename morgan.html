Morgan Wing

Sam Morgan is a New Zealand entrepreneur and businessman known for co-founding Trade Me, one of New Zealand's most popular online auction and classified advertising websites. He was born on August 20, 1975, in Wellington. Trade Me was launched in 1999, and Sam Morgan, along with his partners, played a pivotal role in its early success. The website quickly gained popularity, becoming a significant platform for buying and selling goods and services in New Zealand. It covers a wide range of categories, including electronics, automobiles, real estate, and more.

Under Sam Morgan's leadership, Trade Me continued to expand and diversify its services, including the introduction of fixed-price listings, online job listings, and property listings. In 2006, Trade Me was acquired by Fairfax Media, an Australian media company, for a significant sum, making it one of New Zealand's most successful tech-related acquisitions. Sam Morgan remained involved in various business ventures and investments after his involvement with Trade Me. Sam Morgan's entrepreneurial success with Trade Me has made him a notable figure in the New Zealand business and technology sectors. His contributions to the growth of e-commerce in New Zealand have had a lasting impact on the country's online marketplace.
