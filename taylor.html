Taylor Wing

Sir Richard Taylor is a highly regarded New Zealand film and special effects professional who is best known for his work in the film industry. He was born on February 10, 1965, in New Zealand. Sir Richard Taylor is the co-founder and creative director of Weta Workshop, a renowned special effects and prop design company based in Wellington. Weta Workshop has been involved in the creation of visual effects, props, costumes, and various elements for several notable films, including "The Lord of the Rings" trilogy, "The Hobbit" trilogy, "King Kong," "Avatar," "District 9" and many others.

In recognition of his contributions to the film industry, Sir Richard Taylor was knighted and became Sir Richard Taylor in 2010. He has continued to play a pivotal role in the development of New Zealand's film industry and has been instrumental in showcasing the country's talent and capabilities in the field of special effects and filmmaking on the global stage.
